Getting There

Naples International Airport (NAP)

Flights into Naples come from around Europe and the eastern US.

Linea a Monte del Vesuvio

Trenitalia trains connect Naples Central to Salerno. From Salerno you can reach Ravello via the SITA bus line.

Strada Statale 163

The drive along the Amalfi coast is beautiful, narrow, winding, and terrifing. We'd advise that you avoid driving, however if you do decide to drive we'd recomend you rent a small Italian car.
